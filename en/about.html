ABOUT

Elisa Wendler‑Rabbiolo

Systemic Psychologist – clear, empathetic and resource-oriented. I support people and organizations in enabling helpful movement in complex situations. My focus is on opening perspectives, making resources visible, and developing concrete next steps together – for individuals, teams, and entire organizations.

Short Biography

With a solid education in Psychology, Clinical Psychology and Psychotherapy as well as in systemic counseling and organizational development, I bring experience in counseling, HR, product development and organizational processes. I work with both teams and individuals – on-site in Hamburg or online.

  • Systemic therapy & counseling
  • Communication, leadership and change management
  • Psychological diagnostics and intervention

My Working Style:

  • Structured, transparent and appreciative
  • Context-sensitive and solution-focused
  • Resource-oriented and practically implementable

Attitude – Solution-oriented & Resource-focused

I believe that every person and every organization has their own strengths and resources. My work aims to make these potentials visible and translate them into concrete, implementable steps. I stand by your side in an advisory and reflective capacity, without imposing ready-made solutions.
